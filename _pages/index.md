# Notes that extend your brain
Fleeting Notes provides a quick platform to use a unique note organization method that functions in a similar vein to a brain—through the [[connecting ideas is powerful|connection of ideas]]. This organization technique is [[connecting ideas improves learning|proven to promote learning]] and isn’t restricted by any hierarchical structure. As a result, note discovery is as simple as “[[following a train of thought]]”. 
<video src="assets/fleeting-notes-demo.mp4" type="video/mp4" width="100%" height="100%" display="flex" controls=""></video>

## Features
- Links and backlinks
- Full-text search
- History of traversed notes
- Export all notes in Markdown or JSON
- Add sources to your notes
- [[How to Sync Fleeting Notes with Obsidian]]
